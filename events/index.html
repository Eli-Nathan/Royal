Events

Let us host your event! Our large function hall allows us to host a range of events to fit your needs. The hall is a beautifully grand and historic room which still has an old Masonic Fireplace.

Wedding receptions

Our grand hall will round your special day off in a spectacular way. Let us take care of the the food and drink, allowing you to sit back and enjoy your day.

Birthday parties

Dance, eat, drink and enjoy the party. We can help to organise entertainment, provide food and serve drinks from your private bar. Get in touch to start planning your party.

Corporate Events

Our grand hall is the perfect setting to hold your corporate events. Whether you're simply coming to meet, or you're here for a golfing weekend, we can host all your meals and meetings.

Perthshire Amber Festival

The Royal Dunkeld has been host to the Amber Festival's musicians for the past 5 years. During the festival, which is held annually in late October - early November, we become "The Wee Festival Hall". The music flows through the night and the atmosphere here at this time of year is truly magical.
