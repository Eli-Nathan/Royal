Dining

The phrase "Eat, drink and be merry" comes to life here at the Royal Dunkeld Hotel. With our Restaurant, Bar, Bistro and Beer Garden all serving food and drink, you'll be spoilt for choice. Serving fresh, local produce, we boast probably the best meal in town.

Woodlands Bistro

The atmosphere in Woodlands will remind you of nature trails and our environment.

Larches Restaurant

The larches restaurant gives you a traditional dining experience befitting of the historic area in which the hotel is located with atmospheric photographs of Scottish landscapes.

The Bar

Pre meal and post meal drinks can be enjoyed in our bar where only the finest ales, beers, wine and spirits are served. We are proud to be listed in the CAMRA Good Beer Guide for those who prefer “Real Ales”.

Beer garden

When summer comes around our beer garden is the place to be to enjoy the fresh Scottish air and hopefully a bit of sunshine and warmth.
