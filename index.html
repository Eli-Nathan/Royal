Our hotel

We pride ourselves on our ability to cater for everyone. We have comfortable, cosy rooms for families, couples and individuals. If you are joining us as a backpacker or as part of a stag party, then we have alternative options that might suit. Our Annexe, for example, provides you with comfortable rooms at very affordable prices in a separate building. We recently converted to eco-friendly, green energy with our very own biomass boilers!

Dine in

Grab a bite to eat at our unique "Woodlands Bistro". You couldn't feel more relaxed whilst eating stunning food freshly prepared whilst contemplating our wonderful wooden hand carved woodland animals which decorate our cosy room.

Welcome to Dunkeld

Set in the picturesque town of Dunkeld, often known as "The Gateway to the Highlands", you'll never be bored with endless walks, cycle trails, golf courses, whisky distilleries and some of the finest shooting and fishing locations to be found in Scotland.
